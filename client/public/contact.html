Quick answers

Common questions

How do I track my order?

Once you place an order, you'll see a live status on your orders page. You'll also get a notification when your order is ready for pickup.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel within 2 minutes of placing your order. After that the vendor has already started preparing. Contact us and we'll do our best to help.

How do I become a vendor on QuickBite?

Register with the Vendor role during sign-up, then complete your stall profile. Our team reviews and approves applications within 24 hours.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently support bKash, Nagad, and cash on pickup. More payment options are coming soon.

My order was wrong — what do I do?

We're sorry about that! Use the contact form above or email us directly. Include your order number and we'll sort it right away.

Is QuickBite available on all campuses?

We're currently expanding across Dhaka. If your campus isn't listed yet, drop us a message — we'd love to bring QuickBite to you.
